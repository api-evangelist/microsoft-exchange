<p>In a blog post dated February 25, 2020, we announced plans to update <a href="https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/office/blogs/microsoft-graph-calendar-events-icaluid-update/"> iCalUId on Microsoft Graph</a> to make it adhere to its industry definition.  Since then, many of our customers&#8217; priorities have changed in response to the COVID-19 crisis.  As a result, we have decided to postpone making this update. We will provide the developer community with additional information on new rollout date as we return to normal operations.</p>
<p>Happy, safe coding.</p>
